About Skypiea.

We are a little online shop that sells really awesome, quirky, silly, loverly, funny illustrated clip arts.
We've seen a lot of powerpoint presentations in our time, some good, but most are pretty meh.

The best presentations are ones that can get the job done;
convey your message and ideas in an easy to understand and memorable way.

One of the best mediums for this is story telling though comics. We love comics, we also love funny faces!
We built Skypiea to bring together brilliant illustrations by talented artists from around the world to make your storytelling better.
